Download and view all your important files right on your iPhone, iPod touch or iPad! Save My Files downloads any kind of file from the open Internet and enables you to view pictures, movies and songs right in the app!

Highlights:

  • Download any kind of file to your device;
  • View popular formats, like pictures, video, music and documents;
  • Rename, email and send files to other Apps;
  • Can also connect with iTunes for file transfers;
  • Ready for iOS 7.

For more information and a demonstration video, visit http://lmmenge.github.io/savemyfiles
