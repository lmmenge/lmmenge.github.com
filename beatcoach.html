Meet BeatCoach: the ultimate tempo training app! BeatCoach was built for the musician who's always looking to improve his rhythm skills. Instead of giving musicians fixed patterns and tempos like most of the apps available right now, BeatCoach gives you complete freedom to make your own pattern. There is no pattern builder interface or any other complicated UI. Just pick your BPM and tap away!

Developed alongside the brilliant Michael Loveman, BeatCoach brings you the best of the iPhone experience and the most in music expertise.

Don't miss these great features:

  • Recognize sixteenth notes or triplets! Try the sixteenths mode for the easy stuff and then go into triplets mode and flex your rhythm muscle.
  • Tap your BPM. In addition to changing the BPM one or ten beats at a time, you can tap along to it and BeatCoach will tell you what it is.
  • Score your performance. On scoring mode, you'll tap through 8 measures and then BeatCoach will tell you how well you did. Try to get a perfect score!
  • Hear yourself. Choose if you want to hear yourself tapping or just want to hear the metronome as you tap along.
  • Multi-tap. No limiting of how many fingers you can use on the screen! Tap away in the most natural way possible.
