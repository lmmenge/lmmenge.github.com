What could possibly go wrong with a cookie recipe? Nothing! Unless the cookie wants to eat you instead! Dive into this addictive action game that takes you in a wonderful journey around the world.

Trying to achieve a World Record in a cooking competition can be hard, but nobody ever thought it could be dangerous. While trying to make the biggest cookie ever, the inhabitants of a quiet and peaceful village made a terrible mistake. By using a magical yeast given by the village sorcerer they managed to achieve their incredible goal. Everybody was joyful and the streets were filled with cheering and applause, but soon the excitement turned into terror. The cookie just kept growing!

Save everyone around the world from the terrible monster cookie! Jump over oceans, native animals and other obstacles to get people to the rescue. Just how far can you make it? Find out with Cookie Rush!

  • Travel through many beautifully handcrafted continents around the world.
  • Meet a great variety of awesome characters in each location.
  • Fully computer-generated levels! Each time you play, you play a whole new game.
  • 15 daring achievements for you to try your skills.
  • Find new surprising challenges as the game progresses.
  • OpenFeint: see how well you stack up against everyone else in the world.

Let's see if you'll be skilled enough to save everyone, if not... well, that's the way the cookie crumbles.
